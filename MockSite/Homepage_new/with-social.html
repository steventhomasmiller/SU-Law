The Academic Edge

By focusing on the whole person—mind, body and spirit—Seattle U’s Jesuit education produces highly sought-after students who are well prepared for post-graduate life. The university is a top producer of Fulbright Scholars and our graduates have a 99 percent acceptance rate into advanced degree programs. Prospective employers recognize the value placed on service learning—our students serve at three times the national average—viewing those who engage with their communities as better prepared for professional life and more desirable employees.
