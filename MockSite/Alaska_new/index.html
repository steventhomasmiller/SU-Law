The Alaska Law Campus, part of Seattle University School of Law, offers a path for aspiring lawyers to study the state's compelling legal issues while eperienceing its astounding physical landscape. We offer the most comprehensive legal education program in Alaska, with small classes taught by world-class legal experts.

Immerse Yourself in Alaska Law

Our Alaska-specific curriculum, taught by esteemed members of the Alaska bench and bar, gives you the substantive and practical knowledge to build your legal career.

  • Programs

Gain Valuable Law Experience

Round out your education with a variety of internship and externship opportunities with local law firms, Alaska Native corperations, governmental agencies and more.

  • Experiential Learning

Connect with a tight-knit legal community

Alaska's network of attourneys, judges and legal professionals is incredibly supportive and ready to help you on your journey to becoming a successful lawyer.

Our Program
Our program appeals to all law students at any ABA-accredited law school in the country who want to:
  • Experience Alaska's unique culture and breathtaking landscapes
  • Prepare to return home to practice law or simply spend a portion of law school in their home state
  • Get to know Alaska as a potential professional destination
