Alaska Law Campus

The Alaska Law Campus, part of Seattle University School of Law, offers a path for aspiring lawyers to study the state's compelling legal issues while experiencing its astounding physical landscape. We offer the most comprehensive legal education program in Alaska, with small classes taught by world-class legal experts.

Our program

Our program appeals to all law students at any ABA-accredited law school in the country who want to:

  • Experience Alaska's unique culture and breathtaking landscapes
  • Prepare to return home to practice law or simply spend a portion of law school in their homestate
  • Get to know Alaska as a potential professional destination
